The data

Built from eleven Music League season exports: every submission, vote and comment across 1,001 songs and roughly 3,990 ballots, March 2025 through April 2026.

The method

Performance is measured as a within-round z-score, so seasons with larger points budgets don't inflate a player. Standings rank by share of rounds won on total points hauled.

The genres

Genre and "musical family" tags were assigned by artist from a single editor's music knowledge, not a live database — broad strokes, lovingly argued, occasionally wrong.
